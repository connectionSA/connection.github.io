What is Connection?

Connection recognizes that more effective communication and more effective procedures (to accomplish a variety of goals) would be very beneficial in allowing our industry to flow. We aim to introduce custom mechanisms to aid musicians, arrangers, producers, venues (to name a few) in overcoming administrative obstacles and free up creativity.

Connect service providers through an efficient, online communication platform to put everything you need for your project in one place.

Co-evolve music industry norms to be more transparent, community oriented and in touch with the people involved in it.
